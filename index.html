Our Story

Before we were Cyper4you, we were hardware junkies, computer geeks, hardcore gamers, and everything in between. Building the ultimate system for unbeatable gaming and computing performance is not just a business to us, it is our innermost passion, our purpose – we live and breathe it. It is embedded within our DNA. That is how we came to understand extreme users like yourself so well. Because we are extreme users and gamers ourselves too. We understand the challenge of finding the right machine to suit your specific gaming needs. Because we’ve been there. We understand a system has to offer more than just powerful specs. The design, construction and weight, finishing, future upgrade options, and even down to the last keyboard key, they all have to be in unison. It had to be perfect. Because that’s what we’d want too as users ourselves. We understand. This unique connection we share with our customers has allowed Cyper4you to grow rapidly from our humble beginning as a gaming laptop startup in 2011 to an established customized gaming and high-performance laptop brand it is today.
